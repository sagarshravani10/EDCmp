About Us

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell, CBIT is a non-profit organization run by the students of CBIT that aims at manifesting the latent entrepreneurial spirit of the young students.We at E-cell hosts various workshops,speaker sessions,innovative games,competions for aspiring entrepreneurs and support them by providing necessary resources such as seed funding, mentoring,consultancy and networking.
Launching the entrepreneurship club was like a start-up experience- building a team, creating awareness, getting others involved and doing things we'd never thought of.

The mission of the EDC CBIT is to –

“Develop institutional mechanism to create entrepreneurial culture in academic institutions to foster growth of innovation and entrepreneurship amongst the faculty and student”

Objectives of the EDC-CBIT

  • To act as an institutional mechanism for providing various services including information on all aspects of enterprise building to budding startups.
  • To create Entrepreneurial culture in the Parent Institution and other institutions in the region and to promote the objectives of EDC-CBIT, including programmes related to women entreprenuership.
  • To inculcate a culture of innovation driven entrepreneurship through student projects.
  • To catalyse and promote development of knowledge-based enterprises and promote employment opportunities in the innovative areas.
  • To respond effectively to the emerging challenges and opportunities both at national and international level relating micro enterprises. Functions of EDC-CBIT
  • To organise Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps, Entrepreneurship Development Programmes and Skill Development Programmes in the institution.
  • To organize Business Plan Competitions every year.
  • To guide and assist prospective entrepreneurs on various aspects such as preparing project reports, obtaining project approvals, loans and facilities from agencies of support system, information on technologies, etc.
  • To arrange interaction with entrepreneurs and create a mentorship scheme for student entrepreneurs.
  • To foster culture of entrepreneurship amongst students.
  • EDC-CBIT would also sensitize the management of the institutions regarding the importance of entrepreneurship and integrate their activities with the Host Institutions.

Organisational requirements:

The EDC-CBIT will function under the guidance and control of the Head of the Parent Institution. Its activities will be monitored by an Advisory Board and a Management Committee.

CORE TEAM

The core team co-ordinates the activity body.
